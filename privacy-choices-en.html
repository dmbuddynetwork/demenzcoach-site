Privacy Choices

Demenz Coach works without a user account. The main privacy choices are managed directly on your device.

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Delete local data

  1. Open “For me” in Demenz Coach.
  2. Select “Settings”, then “Privacy & Data”.
  3. Confirm “Delete all local data”.

This removes onboarding, learning progress, check-ins, history, consent settings and saved phrases from that device. Uninstalling the app also deletes its local app data.

Withdraw consent for personal AI assistance

You can withdraw consent under “Settings → Privacy & Data”. No new personal text or voice input will then be transmitted until you consent again. Withdrawal applies to future processing.

Manage usage analytics

Optional app analytics is disabled by default and can be enabled or disabled in the app settings. Website analytics loads only after “Allow analytics” is selected. Use “Privacy settings” at the bottom of any website page to change or withdraw that choice. Withdrawal applies to future analytics events.

Revoke microphone access

You can disable microphone access at any time in iOS Settings under “Privacy & Security → Microphone”. Text input and local content remain available.

Manage your subscription

Apple manages subscriptions. Use Apple Subscriptions to view status or cancel. Deleting local data or the app does not cancel a subscription.

Exercise privacy rights

For access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection or other questions, email dementica@minini.de. Do not send health data. Because we cannot access data held only locally, it can be deleted only on the relevant device.

See the Privacy Policy for full details.
